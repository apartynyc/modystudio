Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does it typically take to complete a website?
Our timeline varies based on the project scope. Portfolio websites can be completed in 3 days, business websites in 1 week, and enterprise solutions have custom timelines based on requirements. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
What is your revision policy?
We offer different revision rounds based on your package - 2 rounds for Portfolio, 4 rounds for Business, and unlimited revisions for Enterprise clients. Additional revision rounds can be purchased if needed.
Do you provide hosting and maintenance?
Yes, we provide hosting through reliable providers like AWS and Vercel. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.
Do you use wordpress?
No, we do not use wordpress. We build custom websites from scratch to ensure optimal performance, security, and scalability. Our websites are tailored to your specific needs and are not limited by the constraints of a CMS.
What technologies do you use?
We primarily use HTML, CSS and javascript for frontend development, coupled with modern backend technologies. Our tech stack is chosen based on performance, scalability, and maintenance considerations specific to your project needs.
